Sage & Stone Wellness Privacy Statement

Introduction

Sage & Stone Wellness ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our clients and visitors. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you visit our website, book services, or interact with us.

Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and any information provided during booking or inquiries.
  • Booking Details: Appointment history, treatment preferences, and payment information (processed securely by third-party platforms such as Fresha).
  • Website Usage: Cookies and usage data to improve website experience and analytics.

    • How We Use Your Information

  • To manage appointments and provide personalized services.
  • To respond to inquiries and provide customer support.
  • To send appointment reminders, updates, and marketing communications (with your consent).
  • To comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

    • Data Sharing

  • We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.
  • Data may be shared with trusted service providers (e.g., booking platforms) solely for business operations.

    • Data Security

  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data.
  • Payment details are handled securely via our booking platform (Fresha).

    • Your Rights

  • You have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data.
  • You may withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.

    • Cookies

  • Our website uses cookies for analytics and user experience. You may adjust your browser settings to decline cookies.

    • Changes to This Policy

  • We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. The latest version will always be available on our website.

    • Contact Us

    For questions or requests regarding your data, please contact: info@sageandstonewellness.com
