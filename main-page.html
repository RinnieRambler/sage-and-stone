meet Claire

With over 20 years of experience in some of the UK and Europe’s most prestigious five-star spas, Claire Vacek is known for her intuitive touch, deep understanding of the body, and wholehearted approach to wellbeing.

Claire created Sage & Stone Wellness as a place where clients feel truly cared for - where every treatment is guided by genuine empathy and an understanding that true wellbeing comes from restoring balance at its roots.

Through mindful touch, grounding techniques, and tailored rituals, she helps each client find a sense of calm, clarity, and reconnection that lingers long after the treatment ends.

Imagine wandering through a Glen at sunrise
—the scent of wild sage warming in the light,the touch of smooth river stones beneath your feet,the hush of nature settling into perfect harmony.
Here, the earth speaks in quiet ways:to breathe slower, to soften your shoulders,to return to the rhythm you were always meant to live in
At Sage & Stone Wellness, we honour these moments.We invite you to reconnect with the natural world, with your body,and with the steady, grounded peace that’s been waiting for you.
